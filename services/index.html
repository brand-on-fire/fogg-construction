Capability Index · 01–10

Every trade the envelope needs. One contractor accountable.

Barrier, flashing, lath, stucco, and plaster — ten capabilities built over 41 years in the waterproofing and plaster trades, available individually or as one integrated scope.

  1. 01 Stucco Waterproofing & Moisture Barrier Systems The barrier behind the stucco is what actually keeps water out. Waterproofing barrier systems installed behind and within stucco assemblies — moisture barriers, building paper, and modern flashing integration that keep Bay Area rain out of the structure. View capability →
  2. 02 Stucco Repair & Restoration Repairs that disappear into the wall — and fix the cause, not just the crack. Stucco crack repair, patching, and full facade restoration across the Bay Area. We correct the underlying cause, then match the existing texture so the repair blends invisibly. View capability →
  3. 03 Exterior Envelope Moisture Infiltration Consulting Find out where the water is really coming from — before you spend on repairs. Exterior envelope moisture-infiltration detection, diagnosis, and corrective solutions. 41 years of experience reading how water moves through stucco and plaster assemblies. View capability →
  4. 04 Custom Stucco Texture Matching Cementitious and acrylic finishes matched so repairs and additions disappear. Custom stucco texture matching in both cementitious and acrylic systems — repairs, additions, and remodels finished to blend invisibly with existing walls. View capability →
  5. 05 Interior Venetian Plaster Applications Hand-troweled, polished plaster interiors with depth no paint can reproduce. Interior Venetian plaster applied by hand — smooth, colored, polished finishes for walls and ceilings in Bay Area homes and commercial spaces. View capability →
  6. 06 Window Installation & Waterproofing Windows are where stucco walls leak. We make them watertight. Window installation, flashing, and stucco patching done as one watertight scope — the most common stucco leak point, handled correctly. View capability →
  7. 07 Stucco Inspection & Correction An experienced read on your stucco’s condition — and a correction plan that fits. Professional stucco inspection and correction: condition assessment, defect identification, and corrective work scaled to what the wall actually needs. View capability →
  8. 08 Flashing & Drainage System Improvements Water always wins against bad detailing. We fix the details. Flashing repair and drainage improvements for stucco buildings — head and sill flashings, weep screeds, kick-outs, and the details that move water out of the wall. View capability →
  9. 09 New Construction Stucco & Plaster Solutions Built right the first time — barrier, lath, and finish as one system. Stucco and plaster for new construction and additions: moisture barriers, lath, three-coat stucco, and finish textures installed as one integrated, warrantable system. View capability →
  10. 10 Commercial & Residential Stucco Services From a single patch to a full facade — big or small, we handle it all. Complete stucco services for commercial and residential properties across San Francisco, Marin, Sonoma, and Alameda — repair, restoration, waterproofing, and new finishes. View capability →
